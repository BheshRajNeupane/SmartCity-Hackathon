Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a critical process that helps treat various medical conditions and emergencies, including surgeries, cancer treatments, and accidents.

By donating blood, you can make a positive impact and give someone a chance at a healthier life.

Donation Requirements

  • You must be at least 18 years old to donate blood.
  • You should weigh at least 110 pounds (50 kilograms).
  • You must be in good health and free from infectious diseases.
  • You should have a valid identification document (ID) with a photo.
